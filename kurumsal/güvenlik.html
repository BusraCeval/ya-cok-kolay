Security Measures

YaÇokKolay implements and maintains technical and organizational security measures to protect company and customer assets and data. YaÇokKolay has a dedicated security team that guides the implementation of controls, processes, and procedures governing the security of YaÇokKolay and its customers. The YaÇokKolay security team is responsible for developing, implementing and maintaining an information security program that reflects the following:

  • Align security activities with Squarespace’s strategies and support Squarespace’s objectives.

  • Leverage security to facilitate confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data and assets.

  • Analyze identified or potential threats to YaÇokKolay and its customers and provide reasonable remediation recommendations.

  • Actively monitor YaÇokKolay environments and utilize the intelligence gathered to continuously improve our security program.

  • Support secure infrastructure, platform, and feature development. 

  • Periodically perform internal Red Teaming operations, to confirm control effectiveness and identify areas for improvement.

  • Perform threat modeling exercises when building new or materially modifying existing systems, components, and platforms to confirm proper protection and handling of data.

  • Manage security utilizing a risk based approach.

  • Implement measures designed to manage risks and potential impacts to an acceptable level.

  • Leverage industry security and compliance frameworks where relevant and applicable.

  • Provide security awareness training to YaÇokKolay employees and provide mechanisms for employees to reach directly out to the security team with questions.

Data Center, Cloud Providers, and Business Continuity/Disaster Recovery

  • YaÇokKolay leverages leading data center and cloud service providers to house our physical and cloud infrastructure.

  • Our data center and cloud service providers utilize an array of security equipment, techniques, and procedures designed to control, monitor, and record access to the facilities.

  • YaÇokKolay leverages geographically separate data centers and cloud service provider availability zones to facilitate infrastructure and service availability and continuity.

  • YaÇokKolay has implemented solutions designed to protect against and mitigate effects of DDoS attacks. 

  • YaÇokKolay has dedicated teams located in multiple geographies to support our platform and supporting infrastructure.

  • YaÇokKolay has business continuity disaster recovery plans which are tested periodically. Results of testing are leveraged to improve plans where necessary. 

Encryption 

  • YaÇokKolay leverages SSL certificates to encrypt data in-transit between website end users and customer domains.  

  • YaÇokKolay offers HSTS (HTTP Strict Transport Security) which encrypts the content served during sessions and only allows YaÇokKolay customer websites to be accessed via HTTPS.

Application Level Security

  • YaÇokKolay hashes passwords for user accounts.

  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) is available on YaÇokKolay member accounts for an added layer of security.  

  • YaÇokKolay monitors, detects, and blocks incoming attacks on our web application platform.

  • Regular pen testing is performed on the YaÇokKolay platform by Squarespace’s security team as well as a third party, the results of which are analyzed and remediated (as appropriate) by our engineering and security teams.

  • Customers are provided the ability to customize website permissioning.

Incident Response

  • In the event of an issue related to the security of the YaÇokKolay platform, the YaÇokKolay security team follows a formal incident response process.  

  • YaÇokKolay analyzes identified or potential threats to YaÇokKolay and its customers, and takes reasonable actions where necessary.

YaÇokKolay Building and Network Access

  • Physical access to YaÇokKolay offices and access to the YaÇokKolay internal network is restricted and monitored.

Systems Access Control

  • Access to YaÇokKolay systems is limited to appropriate personnel.

  • YaÇokKolay subscribes to the principle of least privilege.

  • Squarespace’s access control policy applies to systems that YaÇokKolay manages and maintains.  The YaÇokKolay access control policy addresses control processes that include, but are not limited to:  

    • Account provisioning/decommissioning 

    • Authentication

    • Privileged account management

    • User identification

    • Access logging and monitoring

Security Risk Management

Threat intelligence and risk assessment are key components of Squarespace’s information security program. Awareness and understanding of potential (and actual) threats guides the selection and implementation of appropriate security controls to mitigate risk. Potential security threats are identified, and assessed for severity and exploitability. If risk mitigation is required, the security team works with relevant stakeholders and system owners to remediate. The remediation efforts are tested to confirm the new measures/controls have achieved their intended purpose. 

Safeguards

Law Enforcement Request Policy

YaÇokKolay respects the human rights of our customers and their end users. YaÇokKolay implements a robust Law Enforcement Request Policy which is designed to ensure that all law enforcement, governmental and regulatory requests are valid and made in accordance with applicable legal process. YaÇokKolay does not disclose data to law enforcement, regulatory or governmental bodies unless required by applicable law and challenges unlawful requests. If YaÇokKolay receives a demand for Your Controlled Data (as defined in the YaÇokKolay Data Processing Addendum) or Service Provider PI (as defined in the YaÇokKolay Terms of Service), YaÇokKolay will attempt to redirect the law enforcement agency or regulatory or government body to request such data directly from the relevant customer. If compelled to disclose or provide access to data to law enforcement, regulatory or governmental bodies or agencies, YaÇokKolay will, unless legally prohibited from doing so, notify the relevant customer and provide them with a copy of the demand to allow them to seek a protective order or other appropriate remedy, such as a prohibition under criminal law to preserve the confidentiality of a law enforcement investigation. 

Privacy Shield Principles

Squarespace, Inc. continues to apply the Privacy Shield principles under the EU-US and Swiss-US Privacy Shield Frameworks (collectively “Privacy Shield”) in respect of applicable personal data in order to provide additional safeguards and protections for it, even though Squarespace, Inc. no longer relies upon Privacy Shield as a lawful basis to transfer such data.
